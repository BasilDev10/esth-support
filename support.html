Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enable push notifications?

Make sure notifications are enabled in your device settings for the ESTH app. On iOS, go to Settings > Notifications > ESTH. On Android, go to Settings > Apps > ESTH > Notifications.

I'm not receiving alert notifications

Ensure you are assigned to the alert by an admin. Check that push notifications are enabled and that you have a stable internet connection. Try logging out and back in to refresh your device token.

How do I change my password?

Go to your Profile screen in the app and tap "Change Password".

Can I play games with other users?

Yes! Connect Four, Chess, UNO, and Playoffs support multiplayer. Create a game and invite other users.
